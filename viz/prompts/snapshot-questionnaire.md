# Task snapshot questionnaire (identical at every layer)

Answer ONLY from the material you were given. Do not use outside knowledge of the project, do not guess at intentions beyond the text. If the material does not say, answer "not specified".

1. What is being built, in one sentence?
2. List every required behavior (numbered, one per line, verbatim quotes where possible).
3. List every explicit constraint (style, tools, limits).
4. List the edge cases that must be handled.
5. What is explicitly out of scope?
6. How will success be checked?

Output as markdown with the six numbered questions as headings.
